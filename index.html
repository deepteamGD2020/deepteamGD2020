dear reader      We   the graduating class of 2020 hope you are safe and sound   wherever you are in these times      In these times   our private spaces have become the centers of our public lives. Our common place has dispersed   we found ourselves in our bedrooms   making projects related to social   political and economic issues   We sought to re-enact a shared ground   and finally   we have patched our fragmented times into a shared event   to be experienced in your own time      While they appear as simulations   soon it will also be materialized as a real-time physical encounter   at our graduation exhibition from 10th–13th september in the hague   the netherlands      Your attention   care and curiosity is heard and felt by each one of us by your time spared in this space and visiting our exhibition   You restore a sense of publicness   urgency and importance to our ideas      sincerely      the 2020 graduates of graphic design (ba):      anastasiia umpeleva   anna moschioni   berglind jóhannsdóttir   brechje krah   clara lezla   dana doorenbos   denise jansen   edward dzulaj   emily anderson   fien leeflang   golshan azadokht   hyeonjeong kim   kiki coster   laura brouwer   linda van houtum   louana gentner   marc van den berg   menso jensen   maarten meij   martijn brakenhoff   nedislav kamburov   nicolai schmelling   nicoletta radice   pepijn de jonge   ruben visser   selina landis   seojeong youn   sophia de jong yantorno   tilius sodeika   trang hà   yessica deira   zahari dimitrov   zuzanna zgierska      & non linear narrative (ma):      claude nassar   cristina lavosi   cyan bae   giulia faccin   kert viiart   lila steinkampf   mauro tosarelli      royal academy of art, the hague (kabk)
