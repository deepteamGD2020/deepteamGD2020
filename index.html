Dear reader,


We are happy to have crossed paths with you. We hope that you are doing well and that you and your loved ones are safe.

As the graduating classes of Graphic Design (BA) + Non Linear Narrative (MA) at Royal Academy of Art, The Hague we are raising funds to support the physical manifestation of our works during the annual Graduation Festival of the Academy, which will take place 10th – 13th September 2020. The funds will go towards the material costs of the exhibition furniture, the identity and the signage of our collective installation, and will support 40 students graduating from the Master and Bachelor programmes.

For the making of this video, we decided to bring our works-in-progress to a shared geographic location, each on our time, as a way of coming together during these fragmented times. The title of this year’s exhibition, On Our Own Time, reflects upon how the current condition has had an effect on our experiences of time.

onourtime.today, the site you are currently visiting, will be developed into a multi-chronological online platform where you can experience our graduation works in a mediated environment, following custom interval guidance and tailored curatorial advice.

Our working and living conditions have had an effect on the way we could do research and make work, and also made us question what it means to research and design during a global pandemic. The conditions gave rise to meanings and forms that we could never have anticipated.

Your attention, care, and curiosity is heard and felt by each one of us. By financially supporting us, making yourself present on our platform, and visiting our exhibition in the fall, you restore a sense of publicness, urgency and importance to our work, which is much appreciated.


You can offer financial support via the Voordekunst crowdfunding platform here.

Let us know if you would like to be notified when our works can be experienced on this platform.


Thank you,
and we look forward to seeing you soon.
